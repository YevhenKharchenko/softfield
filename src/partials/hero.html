Soft Sweets Puzzle

Pop the Gummies. Clear the Tray. Hug the Challenge.

So basically, you're matching these cute little gummy bears in puzzles that'll actually make you think. In Soft Sweets Puzzle, you help Candy Friend Coco clear her gummy tray by matching adorable bear-shaped treats. Each level mixes things up with different layouts, and those bordered squares?

They're sneaky - you'll find yourself second-guessing every move. Match three or more gummies. Break stubborn borders. Free the tray and celebrate sweet success. The music's super relaxing and the colors are gorgeous. Honestly, I've been playing this way too much when I should be doing other stuff.

Ready to pop some sweetness?
